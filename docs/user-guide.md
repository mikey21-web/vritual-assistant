## Getting Started

After signing in, you'll land on the **Overview** page. Here's what you'll see:

- **Stat cards**: Total leads, hot leads, conversion rate, recent activity
- **System status**: Whether all your integrations are healthy
- **Quick actions**: Common tasks like adding a lead or viewing messages

## Managing Leads

### Creating a Lead
1. Go to **Leads** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Lead**
3. Fill in the contact information
4. Save — the lead will be scored automatically

### Lead Scoring
LeadAuto assigns a score from -100 to 100 based on your scoring rules. Higher scores = hotter leads.

- **HOT** (70+): Engage immediately
- **WARM** (40-69): Nurture with follow-ups
- **COLD** (1-39): Add to drip campaigns
- **UNQUALIFIED** (≤0): Filter or archive

### Assigning Leads
Click the assignee dropdown on any lead to assign it to a team member.

## Messaging

### WhatsApp
- Incoming messages appear in **Messages**
- Click any conversation to view history
- Use templates from **Templates** to send quick replies
- STOP/opt-out is handled automatically

### Templates
Create reusable message templates at **Templates**. Variables like `{{name}}` and `{{company}}` are filled in automatically.

## Campaigns & Forms

### Creating a Campaign
1. Go to **Campaigns** → **+ New Campaign**
2. Set source (form, QR code, etc.)
3. Configure the destination (CRM, booking tool, etc.)
4. Activate

### Forms
Build forms at **Forms**. Each form has:
- Fields (text, email, phone, etc.)
- Validation rules
- A unique shareable URL

### QR Codes
Generate QR codes at **QR Codes** that link to your forms, websites, or contact info.

## Integrations

Connect LeadAuto to:
- **WhatsApp Business**: For messaging
- **CRM** (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho): For contact sync
- **Calendar** (Calendly, Google Calendar): For booking
- **Email** (SMTP): For transactional emails

Go to **Integrations** to add or test connections.

## Settings & Customization

### Business Settings
Configure your business name, timezone, currency, and working hours at **Settings**.

### Team
Invite team members and assign roles at **Team**:
- **OWNER**: Full access
- **ADMIN**: Manage everything except billing
- **MANAGER**: Manage team and leads
- **SALES_AGENT**: Manage assigned leads only

### Blocklist
Block specific phone numbers, emails, or keywords at **Advanced → Blocklist**.

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Set up scoring rules first**: This helps prioritize your time
2. **Connect WhatsApp early**: Most leads come via WhatsApp
3. **Use templates**: Save time and maintain consistency
4. **Review weekly**: Check conversions and adjust scoring
5. **Test integrations**: Use the **Test** button on each integration
